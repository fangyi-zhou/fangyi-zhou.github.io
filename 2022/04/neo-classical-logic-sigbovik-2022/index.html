Neo-Classical Logic (SIGBOVIK 2022)

 Apr 1, 2022    1 min read

My colleague Martin Vassor and I worked on a paper with title Neo-Classical Logic for SIGBOVIK 20221.

You can read the paper here (with reviews).

In this April Fool’s paper, we explore a possible logic that encapsulates seemingly contradictory observations in the real work. We investigate how such contradictions can be represented in a logic, and show its decidability. Moreover, we prove standard theorems such as “Law of Excluded Middle”, but also novel theorems such as “Law of Contradiction”.
